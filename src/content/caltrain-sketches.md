These are sketches from January 2018 when I commuted from San Francisco to Stanford in Palo Alto using Caltrain. I went to a week-long product management course taught by UC Berkeley at Stanford campus in Palo Alto. I was working at Salesforce at the time.

I drew these Caltrain riders with a Sharpie on a lined notebook provided by UC Berkeley. It’s been almost 8 years; I found this notebook from the back of my closet. I like how the sketches turned out.

Now when I visit the Bay Area I occasionally take the Caltrain for a stop or two. Most riders are still young men working in tech; that remained the same.

I almost wish that the ride was longer so I could draw again.
